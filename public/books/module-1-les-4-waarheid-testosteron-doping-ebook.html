Hoofdinhoud

Defining "Testosteron Doping"

Het term "doping" implies cheating, maar de reality is much more nuanced. We moeten onderscheid maken tussen verschillende applications:

Medisch Testosterone Replacement (TRT)

  • Purpose: Restore normal physiological levels
  • Dosage: 100-200mg testosterone per week
  • Target: 700-1000 ng/dL blood levels
  • Legality: Legal with prescription
  • Goal: Health restoration, not enhancement

Performance Enhancement (Supraphysiological)

  • Purpose: Exceed natural human limits
  • Dosage: 300-1000mg+ testosterone per week
  • Target: 1500-5000+ ng/dL blood levels
  • Legality: Illegal without prescription
  • Goal: Superhuman prestaties en physique

Types of Testosterone Compounds

Testosterone Esters

Testosterone Cypionate:

  • Half-life: 8-12 dagen
  • Injection frequency: 1-2x per week
  • Onset: 2-4 weeks voor full effect
  • Most common: Used in TRT en cycles

Testosterone Enanthate:

  • Half-life: 7-10 dagen
  • Injection frequency: 1-2x per week
  • Similarity: Nearly identical to cypionate

Testosterone Propionate:

  • Half-life: 2-3 dagen
  • Injection frequency: Every other day
  • Advantage: Faster onset, easier to control
  • Disadvantage: More frequent injections

Synthetic Anabolic Steroids (Non-Testosterone)

Common compounds used with testosterone:

  • Dianabol (Methandrostenolone): Oral, rapid mass gain
  • Deca-Durabolin (Nandrolone): Joint health, steady gains
  • Trenbolone: Extreme potency, significante side effects
  • Anavar (Oxandrolone): "Mild" oral, cutting compound

How Steroid Cycles Work

Basic Testosterone Cycle (Beginner)

Duration: 12-16 weeks

Dosage: 300-500mg testosterone per week

Expected results:

  • 10-20 lbs muscle gain
  • Significant strength increase
  • Enhanced recovery
  • Improved mood en energy
  • Increased libido

Timeline:

  • Week 1-3: Bouwen up blood levels
  • Week 4-8: Peak effects, rapid gains
  • Week 9-12: Continued progress
  • Week 13-16: Cycle completion

Post Cycle Therapy (PCT)

After stopping exogenous testosterone, natural production is suppressed:

  • Duration: 4-6 weeks after cycle
  • Purpose: Restore natural testosterone production
  • Common drugs: Clomid, Nolvadex, HCG
  • Critical: Without PCT, testosterone may remain suppressed

Real Risks & Side Effects

Short-term Risks (During Use)

Common Side Effects:

  • Acne: Increased oil production, back/shoulder acne
  • Hair Loss: Accelerated male pattern baldness (if genetically predisposed)
  • Mood Changes: Increased aggression, mood swings
  • Sleep Issues: Insomnia, restless sleep
  • Water Retention: Bloating, elevated blood pressure

Serious Side Effects:

  • Cardiovascular: Elevated blood pressure, cholesterol changes
  • Liver Stress: Especially with oral steroids
  • Gynecomastia: Male breast tissue development
  • Testicular Atrophy: Shrinkage due to suppression

Long-term Risks

Hormonal Disruption:

  • HPTA Suppression: Natural testosterone production shutdown
  • Recovery Issues: Possible permanent suppression
  • Fertility: Reduced sperm production

Cardiovascular Risks:

  • Heart Disease: Increased risk with long-term use
  • Stroke: Elevated risk due to blood pressure/clotting
  • Cholesterol: Altered lipid profiles

Psychological Risks:

  • Dependency: Psychological reliance on enhanced prestaties
  • Body Dysmorphia: Never feeling "big enough"
  • Depression: When coming off cycles
